Step 3: Connect to your Laptop via RDP (Agentless)
To connect to your laptop (or any other computer on the same network as the OCI server) without an agent:

Create a Device Group:

Click on Add Device Group.

Crucial: Change the "Type" from "Manage using a software agent" to "Local Devices - No Agent".

Add your Device:

Inside that new group, click Add Device.

Enter a name (e.g., "My Laptop").

Enter the Local IP Address of your laptop.

Initiate RDP:

Click on your laptop in the list.

Click the Desktop tab.

Select RDP from the dropdown (instead of VNC).

MeshCentral will now open an RDP session in your browser.
